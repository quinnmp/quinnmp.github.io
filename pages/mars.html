Husky Robotics

Immediately upon arriving at the University of Washington, I applied to and joined Husky Robotics, a robotics team that builds imitation Mars Rovers that compete in one or two competitions each year. This team has consistently ranked among the best in the world, and I'm very excited to continue in my role as a software developer for this team.

My contributions to the team so far have been all over the place, from rewriting the logging infrastructure to provisioning NVIDIA Jetsons for use on the rover.
