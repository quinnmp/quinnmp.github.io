FIRST Robotics

From 5th to 12th grade, I participated in FIRST robotics competitions. For the first 4 years, I participated in FLL, which is a program for younger students to learn the basics of designing, building, and programming a small LEGO robot.
However, the most impactful experience I've had with robotics was during my time in high school when I joined FRC team 2910. In this program, students build large 100-pound robots to compete in yearly challenges. My team was one of the best in the world, and I had the amazing opportunity to spend the 2022 and 2023 seasons as the lead software developer. On this team, I learned from talented mentors who are experts in their respective industries. We had mechanical mentors from Boeing, software mentors from Microsoft, etc. To see a list of skills I picked up with my time on this team, see Skills.
To see some of the software-specific awards I received in the context of this team, see Awards.
